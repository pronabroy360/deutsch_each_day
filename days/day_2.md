### 📘 Today's Core Phrases

The 3 most important sentences for introducing yourself:

| German | Pronunciation | English |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| Ich bin Clara. | *ikh bin KLAH-rah* | I am Clara. |
| Ich heiße Clara. | *ikh HY-seh KLAH-rah* | My name is Clara. |
| Ich komme aus Deutschland. | *ikh KOM-eh ows DOYCH-lant* | I come from Germany. |
| Ich wohne in Berlin. | *ikh VOH-neh in ber-LEEN* | I live in Berlin. |
| Ich bin ... Jahre alt. | *ikh bin ... YAH-reh alt* | I am ... years old. |

> 💡 **Note:** *Ich bin* and *Ich heiße* both mean "my name is / I am" — both are totally natural to use!

### ✍️ Practice Exercise

Translate this mini self-introduction into German:

*"Hello! My name is [your name]. I come from Bangladesh. I live in Dhaka. I am [your age] years old."*

**Template:**
> Hallo! Ich heiße ______. Ich komme aus ______. Ich wohne in ______. Ich bin ______ Jahre alt.

Try writing it out with YOUR real details — this is the sentence you'll say hundreds of times! 🙂

---

### 🗣️ Speaking Challenge

Say your full introduction out loud, naturally, without pausing. Aim for 3 smooth repetitions. Your brain learns by *doing*, not just reading!
