### 🔤 The German Alphabet

German uses the same 26 letters as English, plus 4 special characters:

| Letter | Sounds like | Example |
|--------|------------|---------|
| **Ä ä** | "eh" (like in "bed") | Äpfel (apples) |
| **Ö ö** | "ur" with rounded lips | Österreich (Austria) |
| **Ü ü** | "ee" with rounded lips | über (over/above) |
| **ß** | sharp "ss" | Straße (street) |

---

### 👋 Your First 10 German Words

| German | Pronunciation | English |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| Hallo | *HAH-loh* | Hello |
| Guten Morgen | *GOO-ten MOR-gen* | Good morning |
| Guten Tag | *GOO-ten TAHK* | Good day / Hello |
| Guten Abend | *GOO-ten AH-bent* | Good evening |
| Tschüss | *CHUS* | Bye (informal) |
| Auf Wiedersehen | *owf VEE-der-zay-en* | Goodbye (formal) |
| Ja | *yah* | Yes |
| Nein | *nine* | No |
| Bitte | *BIT-teh* | Please / You're welcome |
| Danke | *DAHN-keh* | Thank you |
